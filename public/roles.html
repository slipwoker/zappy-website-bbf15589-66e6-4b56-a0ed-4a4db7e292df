A clear path from listing to release.

Every transaction follows a secure, transparent workflow with identity verification and automated escrow. Here's how Velora guides buyers, sellers, and drivers through each step.

Verify

All users complete identity verification before accessing the marketplace. The system logs verification status and assigns trust scores.

List

Sellers create listings with photos, descriptions, and pricing. AI moderation scans content for safety before publishing to the marketplace.

Match

Buyers browse, filter, and purchase items. Payment goes into escrow immediately. The system matches available drivers for pickup and delivery.

Deliver

Drivers pick up items with GPS tracking enabled. Buyers and sellers receive real-time location updates throughout the delivery process.

Confirm & Release

Upon delivery confirmation, escrow automatically splits funds between seller, driver, and platform. All parties receive transaction receipts and ratings.
