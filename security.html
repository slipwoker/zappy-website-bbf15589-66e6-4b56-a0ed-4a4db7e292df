Security questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about how Velora keeps your transactions safe, verified, and protected.

Why does Velora require identity verification?

Identity verification ensures every user on the platform is a real, accountable person. This mandatory step prevents fraud, protects buyers and sellers, and creates a trusted ecosystem where bad actors can't hide behind anonymity.

What does the escrow system protect?

Our automated escrow holds your payment securely until delivery is confirmed. This protects buyers from paying for items they never receive, and protects sellers from shipping items without guaranteed payment. Funds are only released when both parties fulfill their obligations.

How does delivery confirmation work?

Drivers use real-time GPS tracking and in-app photo verification to confirm successful delivery. Once confirmed, the escrow system automatically splits funds between the seller, driver, and platform. This creates an immutable record that protects all parties.

How does AI moderation handle abuse?

Our AI-powered content moderation system scans listings, messages, and user behavior in real-time. It flags prohibited items, inappropriate content, and suspicious patterns before they cause harm. Human moderators review flagged content to ensure accuracy and fairness.

What can I do if an issue occurs?

Every transaction includes built-in dispute resolution. If something goes wrong, you can open a case directly in the app. Our support team investigates using delivery records, GPS data, and communication logs to reach a fair resolution quickly.

How is role-based accountability enforced?

Each role (buyer, seller, merchant, driver) has specific responsibilities and verification requirements. Ratings, reviews, and transaction history create a permanent accountability trail. Users who violate policies face warnings, restrictions, or permanent bans based on severity.
